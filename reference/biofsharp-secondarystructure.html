SecondaryStructure Type

Namespace: BioFSharp
Assembly: BioFSharp.dll
Base Type: obj
All Interfaces: IEquatable<SecondaryStructure> , IStructuralEquatable , IComparable<SecondaryStructure> , IComparable , IStructuralComparable

compromise summary of secondary structure, intended to approximate crystallographers' intuition, based on columns 19-38, which are the principal result of DSSP analysis of the atomic coordinates.
