DBREF Type

Namespace: BioFSharp.IO
Assembly: BioFSharp.IO.dll
Parent Module: PDB
Base Type: obj
All Interfaces: IEquatable<DBREF> , IStructuralEquatable , IComparable<DBREF> , IComparable , IStructuralComparable

The DBREF record provides cross-reference links between PDB sequences (what appears in SEQRES record) and a corresponding database sequence.
